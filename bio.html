My background

Hey there! I'm a California native, born and raised in the Bay Area. Though my last name is Chinese, I am actually a mix of Chinese, Japanese, and German. I speak English and Japanese, though there are many other languages I wish to learn. (This seems to be what most people are curious about.)

Originally homeschooled most of my life, I (for whatever reason) chose to change into public school and ended up here - UC Berkeley. I am currently studying Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction, and I aim to pick up a double-major in Legal Studies.

Note this is still a massive work in progress!
