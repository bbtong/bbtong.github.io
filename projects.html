Retired Projects

MIRAI Apparel - American-made Streetwear

During highschool, I noticed the unsettling trend that virtually all clothing is manufactured under exploitative and unfair working conditions. Since I was beginning to become interested in entrepreneurial pursuits around this time, I decided to launch a startup to educate against unfair labor conditions and sell my designs locally. All of this clothing was produced ethically and entirely in America. Over the course of a year, MIRAI Apparel grew from a local project to a full-scale online business, and the experiences of financial and logistical hiccups forced me into a more realistic understanding of running a company. As profits slimmed and my interest shifted more towards technology, I felt fulfilled with my project and decided to retire MIRAI. If you keep an eye out around the Bay Area, there's still a number of friends and customers repping the brand - and even a few internationally!
